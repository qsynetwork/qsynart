সংহতি /noun/

Prithibir Pathshala is an initiative by Quarantined Student-Youth Network that seeks to overcome the post-COVID digital divide and increased drop-out rate amongst students. We are trying to reimagine classroom spaces in 75 locations across Bengal so that our students not only succeed at academics but also learn to think creatively, scientifically and rationally. সংহতি : An Art Fundraiser for Prithibir Pathshala is thus a natural extension of the teaching-learning process that we try to inculcate. We hope that you will stand by our students through this fundraiser and beyond, as they grow towards a more equal future.
